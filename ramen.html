ramen noodle soup

home

description

Bored of the same old ramen day-in day-out? We get you. Lucky for you this dish will change your ramen from mid to momentous. From sus to succulent. From aldi to audi.

You will need:

The good thing about ramen is that it's whatever you want it to be, meaning if there's something you dont like in this recipe, you can simply switch it out for something you do.

  • 1 pack ramen noodles of your choice
  • 1 spring onion
  • 3 mushrooms
  • 1 egg
  • 1 stock cube
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
  • kimchi to your liking
  • 1 saucepan
  • 1 sharp knife

The step-by-step...

  1. First, boil the kettle and pour the water into the saucepan. Add the stock cube and bring to the boil.
  2. Whilst the water is heating up, chop the spring onion and mushrooms into thin slices.
  3. Once the water has boiled, add the egg (still in the shell) and the mushrooms as these take the longest to cook.
  4. After 3 or 4 minutes, remove the egg and place it in a bowl of cold water so it's easier to peel.
  5. Now, add the ramen noodles and spring onion to the pan (with the mushrooms still in) and cook until soft.
  6. Whilst the noodles are cooking, peel the egg and cut it in half.
  7. Once the noodles are cooked, add the flavour packet that comes with the ramen, soy sauce, brown sugar and sesame oil. Mix well.
  8. Now, pour the noodles in a bowl, dont worry about excess water, as this has formed a soup base whilst cooking.
  9. Place the egg on top of the noodles and add the kimchi on the side.
  10. Serve with chopsticks/fork and a large spoon for the soup base.
  11. Enjoy!
