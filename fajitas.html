chicken fajitas

home

description

If your anything like me, your probably thinking about fajitas 5+ times a week but you just carnt find time in the day to actually make it. Well, lucky for you, this easy to make recipe is sure to soak up that craving in just 15 minutes.

Vegan? Dont worry about it, this recipe is perfect for all your needs. Just swap out the chicken, cheese and sour cream for a vegan alternative and your ready to go. Now, before we start, we have to warn you because once you try fajitas once, youll want it every day. And without further adue, lets start cooking!

You will need:

Note that everything in this recipe is eyeballed and to your liking, so dont worry about adding to much or too little of something. Just trust what you think is the right amount for you.

  • Diced chicken (or vegan alternative)
  • tortilla wraps
  • onion
  • peppers
  • garlic
  • lettuce
  • cucumber
  • cheese (or vegan alternative)
  • sour cream (or vegan alternative)
  • salsa
  • guacamole
  • seasoning (paprika, salt, pepper)
  • oil
  • tomato puree (yes, the type you put on pizza)
  • frying pan
  • sharp knife
  • chopping board

The step-by-step...

  1. First, roughly chop the onion, peppers and garlic.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan and add the onion, garlic and chicken as these take the longest to cook.
  3. Whilst that is cooking, prepare the other fillings, chop the cucumber and lettuce into thin strips and grate the cheese.
  4. Once the onion and chicken are 2/3 cooked, add the pepper.
  5. Once the fillings have started to brown off, add the seasoning and tomato puree. Mix well.
  6. Heat the tortilla wraps in the microwave for 30 seconds (optional).
  7. Now, we can start to assemble the fajitas. Place the chicken and pepper mix in the middle of the wrap, add the lettuce, cucumber, cheese, salsa, guacamole and sour cream on top.
  8. Now, we are ready to roll! Roll the fajitas tightly, tucking in the sides to avoid them falling out and making sure to keep the fillings in place.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
